Chat Layout — Which column width feels right?

All three use a centred column. The difference is how wide it goes and how the messages sit within it.

Arson & Intentional Damage New Session
What are the elements of arson?

Arson is covered under s267 of the Crimes Act 1961. There are three elements you need to establish:

  • The defendant intentionally or recklessly set fire to or destroyed property
  • The property was a building or structure
  • The act caused damage to that property

Which of these elements do you want to dig into first?
